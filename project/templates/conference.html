SLU's Annual Graduate Student Philosophy Conference

Fall 2019 SLU Graduate Conference Call For Papers

Justice and Philosophy of Religion

We invite submissions for the annual graduate student conference at Saint Louis University, October 25-26, 2019. Papers may be on any topic in philosophy of religion, but papers that explore the conference theme will be given preference. Some questions this topic is meant to explore may include, but are by no means limited to: What is the nature of divine justice?
